# obstacle-puddle.md

A puddle is a comic obstacle because it is beneath Linai's dignity only until it proves itself capable of ruining her rhythm. She should initially underestimate it. Her first reaction is often annoyance that such a small thing is demanding a real decision. Wrong attempts include floating too low and absorbing more water than expected, trying to skim the edge and getting pulled into a weird ripple, or trusting an improvised crossing that fails immediately. Right attempts involve genuine cleverness: hovering cleanly over it, using the puddle's surface tension or reflection to understand it, or letting the water briefly color-shift her body in a useful way. The puddle may splash, ripple, reflect, or reveal something weird about the world, but it should never feel dangerous. Linai's relationship to a puddle is comic disrespect turning into reluctant respect.
