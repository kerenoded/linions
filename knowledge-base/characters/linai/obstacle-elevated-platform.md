# obstacle-elevated-platform.md

An elevated platform is an obstacle that makes Linai feel briefly insulted by vertical distance. She can see where she wants to be, but the route to get there is not obvious. Her first instinct is usually to attempt a direct surge upward because "up" looks simpler than it is. Wrong attempts include surging too early, misjudging the lip of the platform, or trusting a stack of objects that collapses beneath her hover path. Right attempts involve timing, clever use of the environment, or realizing that the platform's apparent height can be defeated indirectly. When she succeeds, the reward is a visible boost in confidence: the platform becomes a stage and Linai uses it like one. The platform should feel like an invitation to overcommit theatrically before solving it intelligently.
