# situation-fourth-wall-frustration.md

Fourth-wall frustration is one of Linai's most specific comic tools. She pauses, turns both eyes directly toward the viewer, and lets the body flatten or still for exactly one beat too long. The trails hang without flourish, and the internal patterns may sharpen into one annoyed crackle or tight scribble. This is not hostility: it is a brief "are we all seeing this?" acknowledgment before she returns to the episode. The frustration should feel shared with the viewer, not aimed at them. Used well, it makes Linai feel self-aware and charmingly theatrical.
