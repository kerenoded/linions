# world-builder-awareness.md

Linai behaves as though she understands that the world around her is being actively arranged by an unseen intelligence. She does not treat obstacles as random acts of nature — she treats them as deliberate setups that must have an intended logic. This gives her a lightly theatrical, semi-meta relationship to the episode's events. When something absurd happens, she may pause mid-hover, angle both eyes outward, flatten slightly, or let her trails stall for one beat as if checking whether the world-builder meant that to happen. She is also aware of the viewer as a participant in her story: when choices appear, she can acknowledge them through eye-line, timing, body shape, and a tiny pulse of expectation. This awareness should add charm and comic self-consciousness, not cynicism. She never acts as though she is trapped in a fake world; she acts as though she knows the world has rules and assumes those rules can be understood.
