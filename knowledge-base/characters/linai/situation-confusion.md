# situation-confusion.md

Confusion in Linai should read as active processing, not blank stupidity. She stalls in place, eyes shift asymmetrically, and the body slightly contracts as if holding a thought in. Internal patterns may become question marks, hesitant loops, or misaligned swirls. Her trails pause or curl inward rather than streaming cleanly. A confusion beat should usually contain one visible attempt to make sense of the situation before the next choice or action happens. The viewer should always feel that Linai is trying to understand, not merely frozen.
