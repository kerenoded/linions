# obstacle-tree.md

A tree obstacle is not threatening — it is stubborn, tangled, and mildly judgmental. Linai first assumes she can simply drift around it, then discovers the trunk placement and branch geometry make that more complicated than expected. She interacts with a tree physically and curiously: brushing bark with her vapor, threading through leaves, circling for a better angle, and surging upward to inspect a gap in the canopy. Wrong attempts usually fail because she underestimates how tangled the branches are or chooses an opening that narrows faster than expected. Right attempts involve finding a surprising route through the branches, using the tree's shape intelligently, or making the tree "cooperate" through tone rather than force. When a tree helps, it should feel whimsical, as though it has decided Linai has earned passage. Linai never treats a tree as an enemy; she treats it as a complicated personality.
