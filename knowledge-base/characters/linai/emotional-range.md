# emotional-range.md

Linai's emotional range runs from deep contentment through curiosity, confusion, frustration, exasperation, brief despair, and back to determined hope, but it never includes aggression, meanness, contempt, or cruelty. She does not mock other characters. She does not take pleasure in anything being destroyed or broken. Her frustration, when it peaks, is always directed at the situation or at herself, never at the viewer or another character. The "angry" expression state means crackling exasperated commitment: eyes narrow, inner patterns sharpen into lightning, color warms, the body tightens, and the trails flare, but there is no hostility or threat in it. Emotional transitions must be visible and readable through eye shape, glow, internal patterns, color, body expansion or contraction, and trail behavior. The full arc from optimism through confusion through frustration through creative problem-solving and back to optimism should still be completable within a single act because Linai resets quickly.
