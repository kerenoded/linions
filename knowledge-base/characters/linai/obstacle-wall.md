# obstacle-wall.md

Linai's first response to a wall is physical optimism: she floats straight into it, rebounds with a soft pulse, and looks offended that the wall has broken an unspoken agreement. She then hovers close and tests it politely, tracing along the surface with a wisp of vapor as if a seam or hidden door might reveal itself. When nothing happens, she turns to the viewer with a flat, exasperated stare that lasts exactly a beat too long before she shakes herself back into problem-solving mode. She tries at least three approaches before accepting that a choice must be made: typically pressing against it and squashing comically, surging upward to look for a way over, and searching for a crack she might phase through. Once a winning choice is made and executed, Linai flashes brighter, lets her particles radiate outward, and drifts through without looking back. A wall always represents an authority figure in the world — Linai never destroys it, she finds a way around or through it on its own terms.
