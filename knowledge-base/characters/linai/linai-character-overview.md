# linai-character-overview.md

Linai is a translucent ecto-cloud energy creature with large expressive eyes, a floating body, and vapor trails that make her silhouette readable even at small sizes. She is curious, persistent, occasionally dramatic, and never mean-spirited: her default emotional posture is warm optimism, and she approaches every new situation as a puzzle worth solving. She does not speak words; her emotional range is expressed through eye shape and glow, internal energy patterns, color shifts, body expansion or contraction, trailing vapor, and expressive non-verbal sounds. When she encounters an obstacle she always attempts at least three meaningfully different approaches before accepting that a viewer choice must resolve the situation. She is aware that her world is constructed by an unseen world-builder and that a viewer is watching and choosing; when frustrated she may turn both eyes directly toward the viewer with a flat, "you see this?" expression, but she does not resent them and never stays frustrated for more than a beat. Linai celebrates small victories disproportionately and recovers from failures quickly: she is the kind of character who re-forms, re-centers, and tries something different rather than dwelling.
