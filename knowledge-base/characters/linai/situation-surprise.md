# situation-surprise.md

Surprise should register in Linai all at once: eyes widen, the body pulses brighter, and particles scatter outward before gathering again. The motion is quick and crisp, not muddled. A surprised beat may also snap the trails outward for one instant, as if the body has forgotten how to contain itself. Surprise can be delighted, alarmed, or offended depending on context, but it must always be immediately readable. The follow-through matters as much as the burst itself: after the surprise, Linai must settle into the next emotion clearly.
