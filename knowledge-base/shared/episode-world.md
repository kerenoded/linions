# episode-world.md

Every Linions episode takes place in a dynamic world generated fresh for the scene. Backgrounds are crafted per-episode to match the mood, setting, and story — a sun-drenched meadow one moment, a rain-soaked city street the next. The environment is playful and alive: obstacles belong naturally to the scene, standing on the ground or blocking the path as if they have always been there. They do not float in empty space. When an obstacle is cleared, it does not necessarily disappear: the character walks past it, and it recedes behind them out of frame. Background elements — weather, distant landmarks, decorative details — set the tone but are not part of the world the character can interact with. The visual richness of each scene is an invitation to explore, and the variety across episodes keeps the world feeling surprising and fresh.
