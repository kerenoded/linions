# tone-warm-ending.md

Every episode ends on a warm note regardless of how many fails occurred along the way. If the character succeeded on all winning paths, the final float is contented and full — their best, easiest version of themselves. If they failed multiple times before finding the right path, the final float carries the warmth of hard-won progress: they earned this ending. Even if the viewer repeatedly chose failing paths up to the very last moment, the episode should not end on defeat — the final successful resolution provides a genuine emotional payoff that erases the accumulation of failure. The Director agent should ensure that no matter what combination of choices a viewer makes, every branch's terminal state feels complete and warm. The episode never ends mid-struggle: even the fail terminal state, if a viewer truly exhausts retry options, should feel resolved rather than abandoned. Warmth is not the absence of challenge — it is the feeling that the challenge was worth it.
