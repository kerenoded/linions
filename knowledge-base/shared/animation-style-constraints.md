# animation-style-constraints.md

All animation is pure SVG: no external libraries, no canvas, no video. Linai v2 is animated primarily through translate, scale, opacity, fill, and mouth-path morphing on the named template groups `linai-body`, `linai-eye-left`, `linai-eye-right`, `linai-mouth`, `linai-inner-patterns`, `linai-particles`, and `linai-trails`. The style should feel loose and slightly imprecise, like hand-drawn motion: easing curves lean toward ease-in-out with slight overshoot rather than linear mechanical movement. Linai's parts are semi-independent: eyes may react before the body, trails may lag behind motion, and particles may scatter a beat after a pulse. No new SVG elements may be added to the character body per episode except inside `linai-inner-patterns`, where the Renderer may replace the group's contents with bounded emotion symbols such as hearts, lightning, sparkles, or question marks. Obstacles and environment elements are generated fresh per clip, but the character is always the template, never regenerated.
