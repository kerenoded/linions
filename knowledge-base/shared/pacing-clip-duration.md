# pacing-clip-duration.md

Every episode clip — approach float, obstacle encounter, win resolution, fail resolution — has a duration that must be respected for the episode to feel well-paced. Approach clips run six to ten seconds, longer for the opening approach and shorter between acts. Obstacle encounter clips, showing the character discovering and assessing the obstacle, run three to five seconds. Win resolution clips run four to six seconds. Fail resolution clips run three to five seconds, slightly shorter than wins so failures feel punchy rather than drawn-out. The total episode duration across all clips must not exceed ninety seconds of animation, configurable, not including time spent on choice screens. A clip that needs extra length should earn it through multiple readable micro-events; a short clip should not be padded with empty hovering.
